Cold Showers

Cold water therapy has many health benefits. A gentle form of stress that leads to thermogenesis (internal generation of body heat),with turn on better immunity,enchance stress tolerance, anti depression and anti chronic fatigue syndrome.

It also helps a lot to improve your willpower.

Also avoid sitting in hot tubs or jacuzzis for extended periods of time.

Light Fasting

Concentrate food intake during an 8-hour window during daylight hours.A 16-hour fast leading to lower blood sugar and insulin levels,and the more intense hormonal response to food, will accentuate the diumal rhythm.

Overweight men are more likely to have low self esteem to begin with.

Avoid procesed foods in your local store,sugary foods and junk foods, go instead for fresh vegetables and fruits and naturlly growth meat and fish, Your body and mind will notice your improved diet in a couple of weeks.
